| 0.24.0 | ❌ Crashes (confirmed by BEATEK) |

Change between 0.11.4 and 0.11.5 that introduced Flash Attention
auto-enable is the most likely trigger. However crash persists even
with FA disabled, suggesting additional changes in the same release
window also affect the code path.
